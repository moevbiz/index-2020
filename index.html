Independent Space Index is a self-organized, comprehensive index of local artist-run and independent art spaces (also called “Off-Spaces” or Alternative Spaces) in Vienna.

Check out the Index website ⟶

The Index acts as a platform of representation for the identically named local association of independent art spaces, formed in late 2017 as a response to right-wing politics and the threat of public cultural funding cuts in Austria.
